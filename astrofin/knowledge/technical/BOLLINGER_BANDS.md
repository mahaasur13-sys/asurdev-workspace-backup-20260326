# Bollinger Bands

## Structure
- **Upper Band**: 20 SMA + (2 × Standard Deviation)
- **Middle Band**: 20 SMA
- **Lower Band**: 20 SMA - (2 × Standard Deviation)

## Signals

### Mean Reversion
- Price touches Lower Band = Potential BUY (oversold)
- Price touches Upper Band = Potential SELL (overbought)

### Volatility Expansion
- Bands widen = Volatility increasing
- Bands narrow = Volatility decreasing (squeeze)

### Squeeze Pattern
- Bollinger Bandwidth at 6-month low = Squeeze
- Squeeze followed by expansion = Big move coming
- Direction usually same as prior trend

### Trend Following
- Price above Middle Band = Bullish bias
- Price below Middle Band = Bearish bias
- Middle Band slope = Trend direction

## Trading Rules

### Mean Reversion
1. Wait for price to touch outer band
2. Confirm with RSI (<30 or >70)
3. Entry on price reversal toward middle band
4. Stop loss: Beyond outer band
5. Target: Middle band or opposite band

### Breakout
1. Price closes outside bands
2. Volume confirms breakout
3. Bandwidth expanding
4. Target: 2× the bandwidth amount
