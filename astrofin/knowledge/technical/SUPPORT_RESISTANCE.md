# Support & Resistance

## Key Concepts

### Support
- Price level where buying pressure > selling pressure
- Previous lows form support zones
- Test support multiple times = stronger support

### Resistance  
- Price level where selling pressure > buying pressure
- Previous highs form resistance zones
- Break resistance = potential bullish continuation

## Identification

### Horizontal Levels
- Look for price reactions at same level
- 2+ touches = valid level
- More touches = stronger level

### Dynamic Levels
- Moving averages (20, 50, 200 EMA)
- Trendlines
- Pivot points

### Fibonacci Retracements
- 23.6%, 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%, 78.6%
- Key levels: 38.2% and 61.8%

## Trading Rules

### Bounce from Support
1. Price approaches support
2. Look for reversal candles
3. RSI at oversold levels
4. Entry: After bounce confirmation
5. Stop: Below support
6. Target: Next resistance

### Break of Resistance
1. Price approaches resistance
2. Volume increases
3. Candle closes above resistance
4. Entry: On retest of broken resistance
5. Stop: Below resistance
6. Target: Next resistance zone
