# Volume Profile

## Key Concepts

### Volume at Price (VWAP)
- Shows trading activity at each price level
- High volume nodes = Areas of acceptance
- Low volume nodes = Areas of rejection

### Point of Control (POC)
- Price level with highest volume
- Acts as magnet during consolidation
- Break of POC = Directional bias

### Value Areas (VA)
- VA High: 70% of volume above this
- VA Low: 70% of volume below this
- Low volume beyond VA = Potential reversal

## Trading Rules

### Volume Spike
- Volume 2× average = Significant event
- Spike at support = Bullish
- Spike at resistance = Bearish

### Volume Climax
- Extremely high volume + sharp move = Exhaustion
- Market often reverses after climax

### VWAP Trading
- Price above VWAP = Bullish bias
- Price below VWAP = Bearish bias
- VWAP as support/resistance
