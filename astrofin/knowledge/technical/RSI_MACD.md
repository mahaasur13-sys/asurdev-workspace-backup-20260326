# Technical Analysis: RSI & MACD

## RSI (Relative Strength Index)

### Interpretation
- **Overbought** (>70): Potential reversal or correction
- **Oversold** (<30): Potential bounce or reversal
- **Neutral** (40-60): No strong signal

### Trading Rules
- RSI divergence from price = strong reversal signal
- RSI < 30 + RSI crosses up = BUY signal
- RSI > 70 + RSI crosses down = SELL signal

### Period Settings
- Fast RSI: 7 periods
- Standard RSI: 14 periods
- Slow RSI: 21 periods

## MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)

### Components
- MACD Line: 12 EMA - 26 EMA
- Signal Line: 9 EMA of MACD Line
- Histogram: MACD Line - Signal Line

### Signals
- **Bullish Crossover**: MACD crosses above Signal
- **Bearish Crossover**: MACD crosses below Signal
- **Divergence**: Price makes new high but MACD doesn't = SELL
- **Histogram growing positive** = Bullish momentum building
- **Histogram shrinking** = Momentum weakening

## Combined RSI + MACD Strategy

### Entry Rules (LONG)
1. RSI crosses above 30 (from oversold)
2. MACD histogram positive and growing
3. MACD line above signal line

### Entry Rules (SHORT)
1. RSI crosses below 70 (from overbought)
2. MACD histogram negative and growing
3. MACD line below signal line

### Exit Rules
- Stop loss: Recent swing high/low
- Take profit: RSI reaches opposite extreme
