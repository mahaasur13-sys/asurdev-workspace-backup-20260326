# Position Sizing Rules

## Kelly Criterion

f* = (bp - q) / b
Where: b = odds received, p = win probability, q = 1 - p

For crypto: use fractional Kelly (25-50%) due to high variance.

## Risk-Based Sizing

Position Size = Account Risk × Risk Per Trade / Distance to Stop Loss

| Account Size | Max Risk Per Trade | Max Total Exposure |
|-------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| < $1,000 | 2% | 10% |
| $1,000 - $10,000 | 1-2% | 15% |
| > $10,000 | 0.5-1% | 20% |

## Session Limits
- Max 3 losing trades per session → stop trading for the day
- Max 5% daily drawdown → stop for the day
- Max 10% weekly drawdown → review system
