## Planetary Yogas для финансов

### Shubha Yoga (Благоприятная)
- Formation: Mercury + Jupiter conjunction
- Effect: Smart investments, profitable trades

### Amrita Yoga (Бессмертие)
- Formation: Venus + Moon conjunction
- Effect: Liquidity flows, market maker activity

### Dhana Yoga (Богатство)
- Formation: 2nd house lord with 11th house lord
- Effect: Sudden gains, unexpected profits
